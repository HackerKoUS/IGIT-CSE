Computer Science Engineering & Applications

Vision of the Department :

To be a place of academic excellence in frontier areas of Computer Science and Engineering to meet the challenges by bridging the gap between academic and industry so as to promote competitive academic programs through research activities that supports intellectual growth and skill acquisition. To become a recognized ethical leader with morale in the state of Odisha and beyond, offering high-quality educational programmes in computer science and its applications.

Mission of the Department:

To facilitate improve technologies and focus on vital services by strengthening the quality of life with leadership and self-sufficiency to create enthusiasm among the students towards nation building. To offer a high-quality education in the art and science of computing, as well as to prepare students for career opportunities in this area requiring a high level of technical knowledge and skill. To develop and administer programs which serve the needs of industrial computer scientists, researchers and computer application specialists for whom the discipline of computing sciences provides indispensable tools.

Programme Educational Objectives (PEOs):

To impart wholesome education with emphasis on theoretical as well as practical aspects so as to produce Computer science and application graduates with innovative thinking, knowledge and skills to excel in postgraduate studies or to succeed in technical profession. To provide the students with a sound foundation in the core subjects of mathematics, computer science and their application areas to make them competent to solve complicated computer science and application problems and also to pursue higher studies and research. To train the students through a curriculum with good scientific and application breadth to enable them to develop analytical skills, multidisciplinary approach and the aptitude for novel designs. To inculcate in students professional attitude, effective communication skills, teamwork skills, leadership qualities and an aptitude to create novel products and solutions for the real life problems. To instill in the students the essence of professional ethics, moral values and social concern and a quest for lifelong learning needed for a successful professional career.

Course Outcomes (COs):

Characterized and design software for various real world applications. Analyze various software problems using various application tools and programs. Solve databases and network problems. Identify and formulate problems, develop and apply analytical and experimental methods of investigation, identify contributing factors and generate, validate and evaluate alternative solutions. Design algorithms for solving real world applications. Communicate effectively in a professional environment through technical reports and presentations. Ability to present fluently and justify technical solutions to diverse audiences. Recognize and evaluate the societal benefits of computer applications. Recognize the importance of professional and ethical responsibilities, the evolving nature of computer applications and the importance of lifelong learning.

Programme Outcomes (POs:)

An ability to apply knowledge of mathematics, the sciences and engineering principles to problems in computer applications. An ability to understand and apply fundamental scientific and engineering principles to engineering problems in the context of the interrelationships between structure, properties, processing, and performance of all classes of computer systems. An ability to design, conduct, analyse, and interpret results of laboratory experiments that involve the behavior of software engineering applications, including the use of statistical and computational methods. Familiarity and experience with modern techniques, instrumentation and other tools require for experimental and engineering design, data collection, and data analysis in the practice of computer science and engineering. An ability to work in and provide leadership for diverse teams in the solution of engineering problems. An ability to identify, formulate and solve engineering problems which involve application and selection of softwares. An understanding of professional and ethical responsibilities and their implications. An ability to communicate effectively through written reports and oral presentations,including preparation of professional quality visual aids. The broad education necessary to understand the impact of computer engineering problems and solutions in a global/societal context. A recognition of the need for and an ability to engage in lifelong learning. A knowledge of contemporary issues in the context of engineering problems in computer science and engineering applications.
